Why a CAIO?

AI adoption is accelerating faster than most organizations can safely govern it. The CAIO role exists at the intersection of strategy, security, and culture — ensuring the business captures AI value without creating technical debt, legal exposure, or eroding trust.

This site covers the complete CAIO skill set: from governing LLM vendors and protecting sensitive data, to running an executive AI rhythm and proving ROI to the board.
